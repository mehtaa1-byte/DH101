# Week 3 Reflection

Reflect 3: What does “authenticity” mean when identity is co-constructed with AI?


The process of achieving genuine identity becomes harder to manage because artificial intelligence systems work together with individuals to create their personal identities. AI systems do not create personal identities from nothing; they use known patterns and common societal beliefs to transform existing identities. People achieve authenticity through both their understanding of technology and their ability to control their technology use. Users who know how AI generates their online identity and control its development process can create authentic digital identities. The concept of authenticity exists as a dynamic process which combines human control with machine determination, instead of existing as an absolute personal truth.
