# Week 10 Reflection

Reflect 10: How does AI change our relationship to play, rules, and narrative?

AI transforms play through its interactive design which allows users to determine their own pathways instead of following predetermined routes. The system creates adaptable rules which operate as concealed systems that change their behavior according to incoming data. The system creates more immersive experiences for users but decreases their ability to see upcoming events and understand system operations. The AI system creates interactive storytelling that changes its content based on user interactions. The flexible nature of this system undermines the established order and the intended purpose of traditional stories which human storytellers create.
