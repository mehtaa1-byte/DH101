# Week 6 Reflection

Reflect 6: What do we gain/lose when machines “read” literature for us?

Machines that read literature for us enable us to read faster while enabling us to discover word patterns and repeated elements and thematic developments that would be difficult for us to find without their help. Our reading experience loses its depth because machines identify patterns through their analysis of texts instead of experiencing actual human emotional states and tonal variations and textual meanings.
