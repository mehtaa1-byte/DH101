# Week 7 Reflection

Reflect 7: How does AI reshape global geographies of power, labor, and environment?

Artificial intelligence transforms worldwide power dynamics because it enables only a small number of countries to control advanced technology with the United States and China as its main centers for major technology companies and computational resources. To develop artificial intelligence systems, companies rely on workers from developing countries who perform essential tasks such as data labeling and content moderation. AI requires extensive energy and water resources because its operation depends on enormous data centers. The world experiences power redistribution and labor movement and environmental impact distribution through AI systems which operate beyond their digital boundaries.
