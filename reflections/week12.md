# Week 12 Reflection

Reflect 12: Is AI sustainable? What ecological trade-offs are we willing to accept?

The current state of AI technology lacks sustainability because its model training and operation processes need substantial electrical power and water resources and physical materials. The operation of AI systems through data centers creates higher energy requirements which lead to increased carbon emissions when facilities depend on fossil fuel power sources. The operation of these systems includes concealed environmental impacts which require water for cooling purposes and materials to construct hardware through mining activities. AI technology presents a solution to environmental problems as it creates opportunities for environmental protection.
