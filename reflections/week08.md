# Week 8 Reflection

Reflect 8: Who is visible/invisible in AI networks? How does visualization reveal or obscure power?

The AI networks create high visibility for major power entities because they control all essential resources which include data and financial assets and research publications. The system operates with hidden elements because essential workers like data labelers and content moderators and resource extraction personnel work behind the scenes. The Global South region contains hidden workers who remain absent from standard network mapping procedures. The most powerful actors in a system become visible through visual representation which shows their central position and ability to influence others. The system brings hidden costs to power while it creates a simplified representation of its operations through digital visualization. Existing power structures become strengthened through network maps which show their operations but they also hide the people who maintain them.
