# Week 9 Reflection

Reflect 9: What is creativity when AI can generate endlessly? Where is the human in generative work?


The process of creativity develops into artistic creativity which leads to artistic production because artificial intelligence creates unlimited output. The process requires selecting appropriate inquiries which investigate essential matters while gathering information that will help create significant outcomes. The work requires human presence because people create the work's purpose and develop the system's guiding prompts and use their expertise to choose which results will stay or go. In generative work, the human acts more like a director or editor than a sole creator. The person operating the AI system needs to provide context through specific elements because the AI system lacks its own understanding of design and purpose. The creative process undergoes transformation because it now depends on decision-making procedures and interpretation activities. I built the bot I experienced this balance directly. I could influence the direction through prompts, structure, and adjustments, but I was not fully in control of the exact outputs. I had to revise prompts and refine the output after the bot created results which did not meet expectations. The process resembled a partnership because both sides possessed shared authority instead of one party controlling everything.
