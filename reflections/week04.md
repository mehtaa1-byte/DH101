# Week 4 Reflection

Reflect 4: Is AI a collaborator, tool, or plagiarist in storytelling?

AI serves as both a storytelling tool and a storytelling partner because it generates creative content and develops story structures and produces written text at high speed. The system depends on established storytelling patterns to create its work because it lacks both personal experience and creative intent. The material produced by AI requires human decision-making to establish its meaning and originality.
